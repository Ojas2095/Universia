# Universia

**Revolutionizing Astronomy in India**

Universia is a thriving community of space enthusiasts united by a shared passion for the cosmos. We bring the universe closer through high-quality telescopes, stargazing sessions under breathtaking night skies, and astronomical workshops.

## About

- 🔭 **Telescopes** — FNOS Series refractors and reflectors for all levels
- 🌌 **Stargazing Sessions** — Night sky experiences at Nagtibba and beyond
- 🎓 **Workshops** — Hands-on astronomy education
- 🎂 **Special Events** — Birthday celebrations under the stars

## Tech Stack

Pure HTML5 + CSS3 + Vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no build tools — fast and deployable anywhere.
