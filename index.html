Our Story

Born from a love
for the night sky

Universia started as a small group of amateur astronomers in Dehradun who couldn't stop looking up. What began as late-night rooftop sessions with borrowed binoculars has grown into India's most passionate astronomy community.

Today, we design our own telescopes, organize stargazing expeditions across the Himalayan foothills, and run workshops that turn curious beginners into confident sky-watchers. We believe everyone deserves to see Saturn's rings with their own eyes.
