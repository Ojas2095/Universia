Our Story

Born from a love
for the night sky

Welcome to Universia, a thriving community of space enthusiasts united by a shared passion for the cosmos. At Universia, we aim to inspire curiosity and promote the wonders of astronomy across the nation.

We specialize in bringing the universe closer to you through high-quality telescopes designed for all levels of stargazers, stargazing sessions under breathtaking night skies, and astronomical workshops to explore the science and beauty of the cosmos.

Whether you're a seasoned astronomer or just starting your journey, Universia offers a gateway to the stars, creating unforgettable experiences that spark a lifelong love for space exploration. Join us, and let's discover the universe together!
